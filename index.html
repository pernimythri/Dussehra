Dussehra Festival

About Dussehra

  • Vijayadashami more commonly known as Dussehra and also known as Dasara or Dashain.
  • It is a major Hindu festival celebrated every year at the end of Durga Puja and Navaratri.
  • It is observed on the tenth day of the month of Ashvin, the seventh in the Hindu lunisolar calendar.
  • The festival typically falls in the Gregorian calendar months of September and October.
